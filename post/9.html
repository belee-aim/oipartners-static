Bally Spring/Summer 2024 Campaign

11/06/2024 Мэдээлэл

Spring/Summer 2024 Campaign​

 

Introducing the Spring/Summer 2024 campaign shot by Alasdair McLellan, showcasing the debut collection from Creative Director Simone Bellotti. Shot within a historic villa, the collection works in harmony with the gentle lines of the architecture, echoing the effortless precision marked out for SS24.​


Despite its location, the campaign setting is pared back in order to let the collection itself — a blend of clever tailoring, beautifully-made essentials and minimal accessories — provide the nuance. The result is an elevation of the everyday, with timeless pieces reimagined as contemporary cornerstones of a 21st century wardrobe.​
