"OI Partners" LLC Signs Memorandum of Understanding with UNICEF Mongolia to advance children’s rights in Mongolia

09/12/2024 Мэдээлэл

"OI Partners" LLC has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with UNICEF Mongolia to support initiatives for children's rights, health, education, and overall well-being. As part of this collaboration, the company will allocate a portion of its sales from its TREX PRO brand of safety and protective footwear to support activities such as measuring children’s foot arch health and enabling distance learning opportunities for children in remote areas.

The TREX PRO brand, manufactured by "OI Partners" LLC, is a premium line of safety footwear inspired by the discovery of T-Rex fossils in Mongolia. The shoes are designed for durability and ergonomic support, ensuring comfort and protection. With a focus on quality and innovation, TREX PRO caters to both industrial safety and everyday comfort, making it a versatile choice for users.

UNICEF Mongolia, under the leadership of Resident Representative Evariste Kouassi-Komlan, focuses on promoting children's rights and ensuring access to health care, education, and protection services. The organization is committed to addressing challenges faced by children in Mongolia, particularly in remote and vulnerable communities, through sustainable partnerships and impactful programs.

At the signing ceremony, B. Nandin-Erdene, Executive Director of "OI Partners" LLC, remarked, "Our TREX PRO brand represents more than just high-quality footwear; it reflects our commitment to giving back to the community. Through this partnership with UNICEF Mongolia, we aim to make a meaningful impact on the lives of children, particularly those in rural areas. We believe that supporting their health and education is an investment in the future of our nation."

Evariste Kouassi-Komlan, Resident Representative of UNICEF Mongolia, shared, "This collaboration demonstrates how businesses can play a transformative role in advancing children's rights. By aligning resources and expertise, 'OI Partners' and UNICEF Mongolia are paving the way for innovative solutions to improve children’s health, education, and overall well-being. We are excited to work together to bring lasting change for the children of Mongolia."

The MoU was signed by Ms. B. Nandin-Erdene and Mr. Evariste Kouassi-Komlan, marking the beginning of a meaningful partnership
