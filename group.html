About our company

THE COMPANY

Welcome to OI Partners! We are a platform dedicated to eliminating middlemen and empowering local retailers. Since our establishment in 2017, we have been occupying 30 percent of the Mongolian shoe market. Our team comprises a diverse group of passionate professionals who bring their expertise and dedication to every project we undertake. We believe in fostering a collaborative environment that encourages creativity, continuous learning, and personal growth. By working together, we ensure that our clients receive the highest quality outdoor brands to wholesaler’s unique needs. We understand that our success is intertwined with the success of our clients. That's why we prioritize building strong, long-lasting relationships. We take the time to listen and understand our client's goals, allowing us to deliver solutions that exceed their expectations.

Greetings of C.E.O

Our company thrives despite the pandemic and post-pandemic economy. We supply all types of shoes directly, bypassing the middleman market. As the first full supply chain business in our country, we create a specialized market for domestic retailers and wholesalers. By localizing international best practices, we offer sustainable business propositions. We offer for a seamless supply chain experience and superior value. Sincerely, Mrs.Nandin-Erdene.B

Timeline

  • 1

    OI Partners LLC was established

    2017

    OI Partners LLC was established in 2017. We opened our first showroom and the store, and started presenting our products. The "Lexter" brand research was completed and the first products were delivered to the market. The first version of OI app has been successfully launched.

  • 2

    Built a shoe factory independently

    2018

    In 2018, we started to establish our own independent shoe OEM factory in China. Successfully exported the first 70,000 pairs of Lexter leather shoes and the casual shoes to Nigeria.

  • 3

    ERP system has been fully tested and implemented

    2019

    In 2019, Mongolian leather was successfully used in the product development research and testing. Successfully organized a business trip to Nigeria to expand the market. Keeping pace with the digital transformation, the OI Wechat Mini Program has successfully tested and fully implemented the warehouse ERP system.

  • 4

    "Oi Synergy" credit card was launched

    2020

    In 2020, a financing system has been implemented to supporting for the retailers. In cooperation with the Trade and Development Bank, the Oi Synergy credit card issued for the first time and started providing financial education by supporting the business financing of its customers.

  • 5

    The Franchises Business has been started

    2021

    In 2021, we offered Franchise business to our clients, enabling them to work in accordance with international standards. In particular, we started to develop product models suitable for rural characteristics, paying more attention to our customers in rural area.

  • 6

    A luxury product was launched to the market

    2022

    We started to work in the luxury product market in 2022. We are working hard to introduce international luxury and high-quality products to Mongolia.
