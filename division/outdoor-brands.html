Above 1580 meters

Mongolia has one of the harsh climates in the World with 4 seasons. The country is mountainous with an average altitude of 1580 meters above sea level, which makes Mongolia one of the highest countries in World. The shortest conclusion is that Mongolians are fans of mountain and outdoor products.

 

Trex Pro®Made in Mongolian Mountains:

Trex Pro is the brand of the hiking, casual, work and safety shoes with a wide selection of high-quality footwear within the requirements of international standards. When we are manufacturing our products, we adhere to high quality standards, exploring comfort, impact on the human health, and selection of superior materials. Advanced technologies such as waterproof membrane protection, anti-slip sole and sole cushioning system are used in product development. It also works with the official Vibram sole system to ensure the safety of users.

Humtto

Clorts®

Our company has officially started selling Clorts brand in 2020. Clorts is a well-established brand in the outdoor footwear industry, recognized for its commitment to quality and performance. With a diverse range of shoes designed for hiking, trekking, and other outdoor activities, Clorts offers reliable and durable footwear options. Their products feature advanced technologies such as waterproof membranes, anti-slip outsoles, and supportive cushioning systems to ensure optimal comfort and protection. With a focus on functionality and customer satisfaction, Clorts continues to be a trusted choice for outdoor enthusiasts seeking footwear that can withstand the demands of challenging environments.
